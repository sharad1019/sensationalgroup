Introduction

Creating a mesmerizing ambiance is the key to hosting a memorable event. At Sensational Group, we specialize in luxury event decor that enhances the aesthetics and charm of any occasion. Whether it’s a wedding, corporate event, private party, or grand celebration, our team ensures an unforgettable experience with exquisite designs and meticulous attention to detail.

Why Choose Sensational Group for Event Decor?

Expertise in Custom Event Decor

We understand that every event is unique. Our team of professional decorators curates customized themes and decor elements tailored to your preferences, ensuring a perfect blend of elegance and creativity.

Comprehensive Decor Services

  • Floral Arrangements – Fresh, vibrant, and artistically arranged.
  • Lighting & Ambience – LED lighting, fairy lights, and mood-enhancing effects.
  • Stage & Backdrop Design – Custom-made stages for weddings, concerts, and conferences.
  • Luxury Furnishings & Props – Elegant seating, table decor, and centerpiece styling.
  • Themed Decorations – Vintage, rustic, modern, Bollywood, and more.

Types of Event Decor We Offer

Event Decor

Wedding Decor

A beautifully designed mandap and stage set the tone for your wedding. We offer floral, draped, and themed mandaps customized to your culture and preference.

Corporate Event Decor

Professional and sleek decor for conferences, award functions, and corporate gatherings to leave a lasting impression.

Brand Activation & Product Launch

Engage your audience with thematic branding, stage design, and lighting setups for product launches and promotions.

Festive & Seasonal Decor

From Christmas, Diwali, and New Year’s Eve to themed parties, we create enchanting festive experiences.



How to Plan Your Event Decor?

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Consultation & Concept Development

Meet our expert designers to discuss your vision, preferences, and theme selection.

Step 2: Venue Assessment & Design Planning

We analyze the venue layout and suggest the best decor elements to maximize space and aesthetics.

Step 3: Execution & Setup

Our team handles all aspects of the decor setup, ensuring a hassle-free experience for you.

Step 4: Final Touches & On-Site Management

We conduct a final quality check and provide on-site coordination to ensure flawless execution.



Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

FAQ

What types of events do you cover?

We specialize in weddings, corporate events, private parties, brand launches, and festive celebrations.

Can I customize my event decor?

Yes! We offer fully customized decor solutions to match your theme, preferences, and budget.

Do you provide event decor services across India?

We primarily operate in Delhi NCR, but we also cater to events across major cities in India upon request.

How far in advance should I book my decor services?

For the best results, we recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons.

What is the cost of event decor services?

Our pricing varies depending on event size, decor requirements, and customization. Contact us for a tailored quote.
